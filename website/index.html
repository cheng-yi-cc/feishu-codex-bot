01 // CORE_MODULES
001

NATURAL_LANGUAGE_CLI

Bypass the GUI. Send natural language straight into your local workspace. Need precise control? Fall back to strict commands like /cwd or /run.

002

PERSISTENT_MEMORY

SQLite-backed context state. It remembers your working directory, branch, and crash logs. Reconnect anytime with /resume.

003

SERIAL_EXECUTION

Task orchestration with strict queues. Prevents race conditions and stops the AI from destroying your file system with concurrent writes.

004

ATTACHMENT_PIPELINE

Drag and drop logs or screenshots directly into Feishu. The bot pulls them securely into the local sandbox for immediate visual analysis.
